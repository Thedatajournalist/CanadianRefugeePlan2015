25,000 Refgees to Canada: How many is too many?

How soon is too soon and how many is too many? The Vancouver Sun has reported the latest data on the target communities of the 25,000 refugees, sourced from the Immigrant Services Society of B.C.. The bubble map here showes the number of refugees to be settled in each province. Large bubbles appear in Ontario Quebec, followed by BC and Manitoba. While the "bubble of refugees" seem notable, it is less than 2.5 refugees for every 1000 Canadians. Similarly, the targets are fewer than one refugee for every 1000 Canadians for Alberta and B.C., where the most opponent voices are.
